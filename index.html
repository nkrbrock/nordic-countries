Get to know the Nordic Nations!

About

The nordic nations are a cultural group of countries and their territories that have a shared lifestyle, history, religion, and societal structures.

Countries

Kingdom of Denmark

Denmark is the smallest nordic country and is the originator of the Nordic Cross flag design seen throughout northern Europe.

Territories

Åland Islands

The Åland Islands are an autonomous region of Finland and has about one island for every 1.5 people.

Wikipedia Official Site

Faroe Islands

The Faroe Islands are an autonomous country of Denmark and has more than half of it's electricity coming from renewable sources.

Wikipedia Official Site

Greenland

Greenland is an autonomous country of Denmark and is the world's island as well as being home to the only Eskimo-Aleut language in Europe.

Wikipedia Official Site

Svalbard

Svalbard is an unincorporated area of Norway with less than 3,000 people, meaning that there are more polar bears than people.
